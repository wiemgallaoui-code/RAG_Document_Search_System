Campus Library Search — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What file types can I search?
A: The demo indexes plain text (.txt) files in the documents folder. PDF support is planned for a later release.

Q: How does ranking work?
A: The system converts documents and your question into TF-IDF vectors, then ranks files by cosine similarity. Higher scores mean more overlapping important terms.

Q: Why do I get no results?
A: Your query might not share words with any document after preprocessing, or the query was empty. Try different keywords such as "neural network" or "SQL join".

Q: Is my data sent to the cloud?
A: This local demo runs entirely on your machine. No external API calls are made during search.

Q: Can I add my own notes?
A: Yes. Drop UTF-8 text files into the documents directory and restart the API server so the index is rebuilt.
